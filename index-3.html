..Chocolate Brownie..

You are at the right place for a delicious chocolate brownie recipe. This delightful treat is sure to satisfy your sweet cravings.

Recipe by: Emily Smith

Servings: 12 servings Oven Temperature: 350째F (180째C) Baking Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (180째C). Grease and flour a baking pan.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan and whisk in the cocoa powder until smooth.
  3. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until well combined.
  4. Add the melted chocolate mixture, flour, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until smooth.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out with moist crumbs.
  8. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
